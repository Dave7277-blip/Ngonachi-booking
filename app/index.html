Who We Are

About Ngonachi_Pix

🎞

Every Shot makesA Big History

Founded in 2012, Ngonachi Studios was born from a simple belief: that photographs should make you feel something every single time you look at them. We've spent over a decade perfecting our craft in East Africa's most stunning venues.

Our award-winning team has photographed over 850 weddings and hundreds of send-off celebrations across Tanzania, Kenya, Burundi and beyond. We bring an artistic eye, technical excellence, and genuine warmth to every event.

We don't just show up with cameras we immerse ourselves in your day, quietly capturing the laughter, the tears, the stolen glances, and the grand moments that together tell your complete story.
