Our Story

Every Shot Makes A Big History

Founded in 2012, Ngonachi Pix was born from a simple belief: photographs should make you feel something every single time you look at them. We have spent over a decade perfecting our craft across East Africa's most stunning venues.

Our award-winning team has documented weddings and send-off celebrations across Tanzania, Kenya, Burundi, and beyond — bringing an artistic eye, technical excellence, and genuine warmth to every event.

We don't just show up with cameras. We immerse ourselves in your day, quietly capturing the laughter, tears, stolen glances, and grand moments that together tell your complete story.
